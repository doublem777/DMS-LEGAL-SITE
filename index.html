About DMS Legal Support Group

DMS Legal Support Group is the intake and administrative branch of Double M Subsidies' legal initiative. We work with The Law Offices of Dave Mooring, providing operational support, community outreach, client screening, and ongoing case management services. Located in the heart of Vineland, New Jersey, we are committed to ensuring legal access for all.

Our Services
