About DMS Legal Support Group

DMS Legal Support Group is the intake and administrative branch of Double M Subsidies' legal initiative. We work alongside Mooring Law, providing operational support, community outreach, client screening, and ongoing case management services. Located in the heart of Vineland, New Jersey, we are committed to ensuring legal access for all.

Our Services

Legal Intake & Referrals

We manage intake appointments and vet referrals from community partners including Storehouse Community Center.

Case Management

We provide ongoing administrative support to ensure legal cases are handled with clarity, communication, and consistency.

Community Partnerships

Working with local organizations, we promote access to justice and support underserved populations.

Collaboration with Mooring Law

While Mooring Law holds full legal authority, we serve as their dedicated administrative and client-facing partner.
